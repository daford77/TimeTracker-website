Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

FordApps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Rental Time Tracker mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data on our own servers. All data managed by the Service is stored locally on your device and, if enabled by you, on your personal cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive or Dropbox).

Location Data

We may use and store information about your location if you give us permission to do so. We use this data to provide features of our Service, such as automatic entry and exit logging for your properties (Geofencing). Location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Camera and Photo Library

The Service requires access to your camera and photo library to allow you to take and attach photos to your visit logs. These photos are stored on your device and are only shared if you choose to export them or send them via email/SMS.

Contact Information

The Service allows you to store contact information for property owners and tenants. This data is used solely for the purpose of the app's features (such as notifying owners of a visit) and is never shared with third parties by us.

2. Storage and Security

All data created within the app is stored in a private database on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures provided by the Android operating system to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

3. Cloud Backup (Pro Feature)

If you choose to use the Automated Cloud Backup feature, your data will be encrypted and stored in your own personal cloud storage account. We do not have access to these files or your cloud credentials.

4. Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service, such as:

  • Google Play Services (for location and maps)
  • Firebase (for analytics and crash reporting, if enabled)

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
